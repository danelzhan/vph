Volunteer

Volunteers are essential to our mission. With a variety of benefits, such as making a positive impact on Vancouver’s underprivileged community, meeting new people, and learning about the pressing crises of food insecurity and homelessness, volunteering with Vancouver Packages for Hope provides a one-of-a-kind experience to those who are interested.
